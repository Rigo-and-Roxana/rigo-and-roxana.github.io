Our Story

June 2019

The First Meeting

We met at a friend's coffee shop in Capitol Hill. Michael spilled his latte on Sarah's notebook, and instead of getting angry, she laughed. We talked for three hours that day.

December 2021

The First Adventure

Our first big trip together was to the Oregon Coast during a winter storm. We got caught in the rain, built a fire in our rented cabin, and knew we were meant to be.

August 2023

The Proposal

On a hike to Rattlesnake Ledge at sunset, Michael got down on one knee. Sarah said "yes" as the sun dipped behind the mountains. It was perfect.
